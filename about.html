Archaeological Museum: Днес Варненският археологически музей е сред най–големите музеи в страната. В него се съхраняват едни от най–забележителните и ценни паметници на световната култура. Той се превърна в културен и научен център с много висококвалифицирани специалисти. Музеят и неговите колекции представят България на десетки национални и международни изложби.
SEA Garden: Varna, often referred to as the "Sea Capital of Bulgaria," is a vibrant city located on the Black Sea coast. It offers a rich tapestry of history, culture, and natural beauty, making it a must-visit destination for travelers.
Aladzha Monastery: The monastery caves were hewn into a 25-m high vertical karst cliff near the upper edge of the Franga Plateau on several levels. The complex includes two small nearby catacombs. Dedicated to the Holy Trinity, it was an active hesychast monastic community of the Second Bulgarian Empire since the 12th century and perhaps survived until the early 18th century. Nearby, remains of a 5th-century cave monastery have been found.
Aquarium Varna: The aquarium's exhibition focuses on the Black Sea's flora and fauna which includes over 140 fish species, but also features freshwater fish, Mediterranean fish, exotic species from faraway areas of the World Ocean, mussels and algae.
Varna Dolphinarium: Open since 1984, the Dolphinarium has been one of Varna's top attractions. The aluminum and glass building, which itself has an unique outfit, is home for the lovely dolphins of a Carribean sea breed. The unique dolphin show includes acrobatics,dolphin dances, music and balance.
Asparuhov Bridge: The Asparuhov most (Bulgarian: Аспарухов мост) or Asparuhov Bridge is a bridge in Varna on the Black Sea coast of Bulgaria. It connects the Asparuhovo district to the rest of the city over the canals between the Black Sea and Lake Varna.
Varna Retro Museum: Das ganze Leben aus der Zeit des Sozialismus kommt zum Leben in das neue Retro Museum. Im Jahr 2015, am Tag der Arbeiterbewegung - 1. Mai, öffnet in Varna offiziell das einzigartige für Bulgarien "Retro Museum".
